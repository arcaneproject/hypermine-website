Transparent Government

Eklektos – is a blockchain based secure, next generation, tamper and hack proof government election solution, which is not only mobile [on smartphones], but can also be ported to a variety of offline hardware to reach remote locations.

The platform is transparent - Using ‘Distributed Ledger Technology’; our application is quick to deploy and allows citizens to vote in a fraction of the time generally required to vote, eliminating long queues and cumbersome processes. The platform is flexible to and can be adapted to all forms of elections, from by-polls to state elections; internal assembly consensus and also for national elections.
